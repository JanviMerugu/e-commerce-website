Rose and fig Lipbalm.

Rose and Fig Lip Balm is a luxurious lip treatment that blends the hydrating benefits of rose oil with the nourishing properties of fig extract. This balm deeply moisturizes and repairs dry, chapped lips, leaving them soft and supple. Rose oil soothes and revitalizes, while fig extract provides added nourishment and antioxidants. The result is a smooth, hydrated lip with a subtle, natural sheen. Lightweight and non-greasy, it offers long-lasting comfort and a touch of indulgence for beautifully cared-for lips.
